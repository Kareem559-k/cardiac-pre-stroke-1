Overview

From raw ECG signals to an early-warning risk signal ‚Äî designed for continuous monitoring and clinical clarity.

Cardiovascular diseases and stroke are among the leading causes of death worldwide, often occurring without early warning symptoms. Subtle electrical micro-dynamic changes can precede critical events, yet remain invisible to standard short ECG snapshots.

This project proposes an AI-synchronized ECG workflow that extracts micro-dynamics, learns latent risk patterns, and produces an interpretable risk score for early preventive action ‚Äî especially in CAD patients.
